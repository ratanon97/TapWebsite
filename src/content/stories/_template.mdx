## The situation

Set the stage. What was the project, the constraint, the deadline? Keep it specific — the reader should picture it.

## The tension

What made this hard? Name the real friction, not the sanitised version that went into the postmortem.

## What I did

Walk through the process step by step. Don't skip to the result — the process is what AI can't replicate.

## What I learned

One or two sentences. The lesson future-you (or a reader in a similar spot) should actually hold on to.
